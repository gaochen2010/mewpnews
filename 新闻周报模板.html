V. Synthesis & Takeaways

  1. Electrification + Zero-Emission Power are “rental-ready”: Hydrogen/electric temporary power combined with electric MEWPs is being adopted; contractors evaluate total TCO at project level.
  2. European rental consolidation accelerates: Transactions and large orders in Germany/Nordics/France reflect constructive expectations for 2026–2027.
  3. OEM localization and service radius matter: Direct service, parts, and training networks in Brazil and the Middle East materially improve delivery and maintenance experience.
  4. Industrial megaprojects concentrate demand: Data centers, nuclear, rail, and gigafactories drive sustained needs for scissors and mid/high-reach booms; specialty niches (bridge/tunnel) deepen.
  5. Regulation lifts the bar on “soft power”: Safety records, remote monitoring, and compliance documentation are critical for equipment circulation and tendering.

  3. OEM 本地化与服务半径成为竞争焦点：巴西与中东直达服务、备件与培训网络完善，显著改善交付与维保体验。
